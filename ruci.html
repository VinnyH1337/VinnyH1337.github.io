RUCI Europe Index

The Russia-Ukraine Conflict Uncertainty (RUCI) Europe Index measures the intensity of uncertainty associated with the Russia-Ukraine conflict in European news coverage. Higher values indicate periods in which conflict-related uncertainty is more prominent.

The monthly series makes it easier to compare changes over time and to identify major increases around turning points in the conflict. The index is intended as a research tool for studying how geopolitical uncertainty relates to financial markets and the wider European economy.
